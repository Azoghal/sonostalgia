About Sonostalgia

Why I Wanted to Make This

Hearing a song that instantly conjures a vivid memory is special. Even more so when it's a specific moment that you've not thought of in years.

I often joke that I have a sieve brain. So many great moments that I've forgotten. Some of those moments can be found again with the right song.

I wanted somewhere to record those songs that take me back, so I decided to build it.
